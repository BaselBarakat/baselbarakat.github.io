About Me

I am Head of Computer Science and AI at Goldsmiths, University of London, where I hold strategic and operational responsibility for seven degree programmes serving approximately 500 on-campus undergraduates, 50 postgraduate students, and 5,000 online learners globally. I lead a subject area of 28 academic staff with responsibility for curriculum development, quality assurance, and programme accreditation.

My research spans Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Internet of Things, and next-generation communication systems — with a particular focus on healthcare innovation, renewable energy, and ethical AI. I secure funding from the NHS, EU, and EPSRC for translational projects that bridge research and real-world impact.

Research Areas

🤖

Artificial Intelligence & ML

Large language models, fine-tuning strategies, forecasting algorithms, and block-wise optimisation for deep learning.

🌐

6G & IoT Systems

Next-generation wireless communication, real-time IoT architectures, and AI-driven network design.

Healthcare AI

ML-driven demand forecasting for healthcare services, Alzheimer's detection via acoustic analysis, and clinical data modelling.

♻️

Sustainable Energy

AI forecasting for hybrid renewable energy systems, coupled thermo-electrical dispatch strategies, and grid optimisation.

🛡️

Ethical AI & NLP

Evaluating LLMs for hate speech detection, empirical dataset analysis, and responsible AI deployment frameworks.

📐

Mathematical Modelling

Real-time system modelling, communication systems engineering, and applied mathematical frameworks for complex systems.
